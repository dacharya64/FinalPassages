VoyageID = our unique identification number for each voyage. 
YearAM = the year of the voyage
Rig = the type of ship 
ShipName = the ship’s name, of course
CaptainA = the ship’s captain at the outset of the voyage (usually for the whole voyage)
Slaximp = the estimated number of captives on board at the vessel’s departure from Jamaica
MJSELIMP = the colony where we believe most (usually all) captives disembarked from the voyage
